Bidding Tic-Tac-Toe

This page in Русский.

Telegram bot

You can play Bidding Tic-Tac-Toe in Telegram bot @BiddingTicTacToeBot.

Game rules

  • Each player gets 100 tokens at start of the game
  • To win right to make next turn players make bets
  • The one who bid more makes the turn. If bids are equal the player with the bigger stack makes the turn. If both bids & balance are equal the turn makes player who first placed a bid.
  • The one who made the turn pass his bid to another player.
  • The one who builds a line from side to side or from corner to corner becomes the winner.

Open source!

This game is written in Go language using Strongo Bots Framework.
